## How to Choose Based on Your Needs

### Choose FreshBooks if:
- You want the most professional-looking invoices
- You need integrated time tracking and expense management
- Your clients appreciate premium presentation

### Choose Wave if:
- You're on a tight budget but need professional features
- You want unlimited free invoicing
- You don't need advanced accounting features yet

### Choose QuickBooks if:
- You're building a business, not just freelancing
- You need double-entry accounting and detailed reporting
- You plan to add employees or complex business structures

## Implementation Tips for Faster Payments

1. **Set Up Recurring Invoices** — Automate retainer or subscription billing
2. **Enable Online Payments** — Clients pay 3x faster with credit card options
3. **Use Professional Templates** — Branded invoices get taken more seriously
4. **Configure Auto-Reminders** — Gentle nudges reduce late payments
5. **Offer Multiple Payment Methods** — Credit cards, bank transfer, PayPal options
